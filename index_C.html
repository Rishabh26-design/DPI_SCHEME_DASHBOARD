Frequently Asked Questions

All students studying in government schools from class 1 to 8 are eligible for the free uniform scheme. You can verify your child's enrollment status by entering their admission number on our student portal or contacting your school principal.

For the bicycle scheme, you need to submit: 1) Aadhaar card copy of the student, 2) Bonafide certificate from school, 3) Caste certificate (if applicable), and 4) Latest mark sheet. All applications are processed through the school administration.

The dashboard data is updated in real-time as schools submit their reports. Financial data is updated weekly, while beneficiary numbers are updated daily. The "Last Updated" timestamp at the top of each section indicates when that particular data was refreshed.

You can report any issues through our online grievance portal available in the contact section, or call our 24x7 helpline at 1800-123-4567. For district-specific issues, you may also contact the District Education Officer whose details are available under each district's profile.
